MORE THAN JUST A HOT DOG

OUR STORY

Top Dog grew out of a boy's love of sausage, a staple in his German immigrants' New York home over the WWII years. His paper route to a well-mixed neighborhood assured that Italian, Polish, even Hungarian sausages were soon no strangers to that developing appetite and palate. Nor had he far to go to a cart or stand offering kosher style "Franks", usually steeped but better off the griddle.
