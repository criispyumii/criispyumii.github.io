Plant Care

indoor plants...

come in all different shapes and sizes! this means that each one may need to be watered more or less frequently. It's important to feel the soil to determine if it's ready to be watered again!

sunlight...

is a very important factor when it comes to plant growth and happiness. Make sure to research the amount of sunlight that's optimal for your plant!

repotting...

may be necessary after you've had your plant for a while. Check the roots every so often, and if they're circling the container, it's time to get a bigger pot.

pruning...

your plants helps keep your plants looking healthy and full. Leaving dead leaves on the stems and at the base of your plant can cause crowding and discourage growth.

propagating..

is a great way to turn clippings into full grown plant buddies! This is a great way to save money, since you won't have to buy new plants. You can essentially duplicate your current ones!
